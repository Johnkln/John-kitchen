Classic Pizza Dough

    Ingredients:

  • 1 kilograms/14 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 4-5 grams/1.5 tsp of dry yeast
  • 20 grams/1.5 tbsp of sugar
  • 20 grams/3 tsp of salt
  • 20 grams/1.5 tbsp of oil
  • 600-700 ml warm water (around 40-50°C)

    Procedure:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.

  2. Add the oil and mix again until the oil is evenly distributed throughout the flour mixture.

  3. Gradually pour the warm water into the bowl while mixing the dough with a wooden spoon or a dough hook attachment on a stand mixer. Continue adding water until the dough comes together and is slightly sticky to the touch.

  4. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes by hand on a lightly floured surface you can look up on how to knead a dough here or with a dough hook attachment on a stand mixer. The dough should be smooth and elastic.

  5. Lightly oil a large bowl and place the dough inside, turning it over to coat it in oil. Cover the bowl with a damp towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

  6. After the dough has risen, gently punch it down and divide it into equal portions, depending on what you plan to make with it(in our case, pizza so 5 equal portions if enough).

  7. Flatten the dough into desired shape and size

  8. Pre-cook it in a 450°C pre-heated oven for 4 minutes.
